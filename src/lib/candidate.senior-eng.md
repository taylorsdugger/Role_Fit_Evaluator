# Candidate Profile — Senior Product Engineer

## Summary
Senior Software Engineer with 8+ years of experience designing, building, and scaling customer-facing and backend systems in production B2B environments. Strong focus on reliability, performance, testability, and operational excellence. Experienced technical lead who works closely with product and design while coaching engineers and driving delivery quality.

## Core Strengths
- Owning systems end-to-end from design through production support
- Translating product requirements into scalable, maintainable solutions
- Improving system reliability, performance, and observability
- Raising engineering quality through testing, standards, and process
- Leading teams without relying on authority or heroics
